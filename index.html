The checkpoint is reached through BAU organic trend + initiative lift. The committed September number is 11,241. Blog remains a traffic and lead-generation channel first. The expected outcome is ~1,000 leads/month by September, feeding a nurture engine worth ~120–240 trials/year. That matters to the plan, but blog is not the lever that closes a final September gap on its own. /our-menu does that.

Why our target is achievable — a distribution problem, not a ceiling

Our conservative target (1.4%) is already beaten on 6+ of our pages. Our ambitious target (2.3%) is already beaten by our top 3 pages. The 0.47% overall rate is a distribution and infrastructure problem — 276 low-intent pages drag our average down. The path to 12K/year is making our top 50 pages perform more like our top 10. The breakout case at 3–4% is a different beast: that requires bigger offer quality, sharper intent matching, and broader rollout of the highest-converting page patterns.

Device context: 74% mobile

74% of our blog traffic is mobile web (some pages exceed 80%). Exit-intent overlays do not reliably fire on mobile browsers. All of our CTA infrastructure must be scroll-triggered (fire at 40–60% scroll depth) or in-content. In GA, we measure blog leads with the landing_popup_submitted event. That is the consistent event behind the popup lead rate used throughout this plan.

Appendix

Data and assumptions

This section contains the data foundation, forecast logic, and key assumptions behind the plan.

Data foundation and forecast logic
Source of truth

Historical performance is based on organic trials from GOLD_MARKETING.MARKETING_TRACKER, filtered to PRIMARY_CHANNEL = 'Organic' and excluding resurrected users.

Verified actuals used in this plan: 2025 full year = 105,878 trials and 2026 Q1 = 33,392 trials.

Lead measurement on blog

In this plan, we call blog captures leads. The primary lead event used in GA is landing_popup_submitted, which measures popup-based lead capture on blog surfaces.

That means blog lead rate is read as landing_popup_submitted divided by sessions. Inline or gated lead mechanisms can be layered in later, but popup capture is the consistent baseline used here.

Projection model

The forecast starts with a trend-only baseline of 128,742 trials, then layers in incremental impact from initiatives as they launch and scale through the year. Initiative impact is annualized at steady state, but only part of that annualized value is assumed to land in 2026.

The updated full-year plan range is ~134,800–136,800 trials, based on an added ~1,500–4,000 trials from that 9th initiative in 2026. In the operating view for the next 12 months, the same system scales to a ~14.7K–21.2K annual run-rate once fully ramped.

How to read the numbers

The September checkpoint remains 11,241. The prior monthlyized planning view was ~11,795. With purchase-intent landing page system added, the September planning range extends to ~11,945–12,195, reflecting a ~150–400 trials/month September ramp from the 9th initiative.

What is verified vs. planned

Historical actuals are Snowflake-verified. Forward-looking values are planning projections built from that baseline, expected rollout timing, and modeled initiative contribution.

In practice: historical actuals are factual, while 2026 totals and checkpoint values are forecasted operating targets.
